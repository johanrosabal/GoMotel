# **App Name**: Go Motel Manager

## Core Features:

- Room Check-in: Manage room check-ins, recording entry time and guest details.
- Room Check-out: Handle room check-outs, calculating the total stay duration and generating the bill.
- Service Ordering: Allow guests or staff to order services (food, drinks, etc.) for a specific room. Order should contain all items the guest wishes to acquire.
- Inventory Management: Track the inventory of available rooms and services. Increment and decrement the available service upon purchase. Indicate a room's availabilty on check-in/check-out
- Billing: Calculate bills based on room rates and ordered services. Track what services where provided.
- Realtime Updates: Employ a tool to automatically keep track of services, room availability, and order status for rooms and services across all components.
- Firestore integration: Use Firestore as the database to store and manage room information, services, inventory, and transactions.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep purple (#673AB7) to convey luxury and relaxation, aligning with the hospitality vibe.
- Background color: Light gray (#EEEEEE) to ensure content readability and a modern, clean aesthetic.
- Accent color: Teal (#009688) to highlight important actions and information, contrasting with the purple.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif known for its modern and neutral look, suitable for both headlines and body text.
- Use simple, clear icons for room status, service types, and actions to aid navigation and quick understanding.
- Design a clean and intuitive layout, prioritizing easy navigation and information clarity for staff and potentially for guest-facing interfaces.
- Incorporate subtle transitions and feedback animations for interactions (e.g., form submissions, status changes) to improve user experience.
